What is PATH?

Our team created PATH, short for Planned Assignment Timeline Helper, to aid and encourage students to keep track of their assignments, quizzes, and exams and help them get everything done on time! This timeline tool utilizes a unique way of displaying these events to show the student what's coming up, how long it will take, and how many other events they need to prepare for.

How to use PATH

To use PATH, please select the Events tab at the top of the page and begin entering your events! Once you have finished entering as many items as you would like, select the "Done" button and your timeline will be created and displayed.
