What is PATH?

Our team created PATH, short for Planned Assignment Timeline Helper, to aid and encourage students to keep track of their assignments, quizzes, and exams and assist them in completing these tasks on time! This timeline tool utilizes a unique way of displaying these events to inform the student of what's coming up, how long it will take, and what other events they need to prepare for.

How to use PATH

To use PATH, select the Events tab at the top of the page and begin entering the details of your events. Once you have finished entering as many items as you would like, select the "Save Event" button and it will be added to your timeline. You can then press the "View Timeline" button or navigate to the Timeline tab to display the timeline that has been generated from your saved events.
